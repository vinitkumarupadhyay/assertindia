About Us

This is what we do.

Write for India initiative is a platform to disseminate information, ideate solutions and instill strength to core values of the concept of India. This initiative will foster a range of thought leaders in different fields such as economics, society, history, literature, politics, science, philosophy, sports and environment. We hope to host verified information, innvotive ideas and inspirational thoughts and opinions in different arenas. India is intricately interwoven with the world hence we will not restrict ourselves in going beyond the borders however focus will remain on India. All articles will be relevant to the rise and holistic socio-economic development and betterment of India.

This iniative will provide writers a stage for recognition and healthy competition. Content will be edited and verified with utmost care. Opinions and thought will be free but they will belong solely to the author.
